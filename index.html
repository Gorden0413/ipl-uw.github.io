Information Processing Lab


University of Washington    Seattle, WA


Main research thrust of our current work is in the multimedia signal processing, multimedia networking and machine learning areas. A specific focus is on the large scale smart camera networks analytics and networking.

The research group works under the guidance of Prof. Jenq-Neng Hwang and Prof. Ming-Ting Sun.
